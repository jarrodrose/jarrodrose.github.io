Current thoughts about Midsumma at Gasworks Arts Park so far - pretty great. I am the Venue Technicians and Operator for one of the shows, *Beyond Prescilla*. No one actually follows this website, otherwise I would tell everyone to go see it.

It's a play written by Gavin Roach, based on a book by Daniel Witthaus, directed by Lauren Hopley with four wonderfully talented actors, Kevin Dee, Jane Barry, Danny Carroll & Emma Jo McKay.

The story follows Daniel's journey around Australia talking to LGBTI individuals in regional and rural parts of the country, and how he came to start the whole endevour.

The experience has been great for me so far. I have enjoyed having a more manual lighting desk to play with, as I am currently used to the *ETC Ion* that can pre-program all steps and just requires pressing the go button for each cue. The desk I am using, the *LSC Minim* is less forgiving and required manual intervention for some cues as timing cannot be so accurately programmed. (At least not that I have found so far. There are ways to program, but it doesn't seem to allow for Fade In/ Snap Out of cues. I might be doing it wrong, still learning.)
