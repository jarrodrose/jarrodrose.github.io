I just want to say how much I hate moving house. All of my side projects have to be put on hold, I basically only work, sleep and unpack for about two weeks. Then it takes another month to get the workshop setup so I can begin creating again. It's such a draining time. It pulls at all the parts of your life; Relationships, work & health all suffer during this time.

I have so many things I want to do. So many things I want to create. But after I have worked my 10 hours today, I will go home and unpack more boxes so that in a few weeks I can make the things that keep me sane.

I am aware how privileged and first-world-problem-y this is. I have a home. I can pay the rent on my home. I eat. I eat badly but I eat. I have enough money to eat badly.

I just miss my friends, and my art.
