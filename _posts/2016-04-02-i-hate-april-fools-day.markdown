I don't really hate it. I just hate the melicious intentions some people have regarding this 'festival' of horrors.

I know I must sounds like a kill-joy, but I wish people would just paste "April Fools!" at the bottom of their internet pages on the topic.

I'm not even complaining because someone/something tricked me. I never assume anything is true for the approx 48 hours
from it being April 1st in Australia, to it being April 1st in Hawaii.

It was cute to begin with internet. But I'm done. I'm also an old grump who should re-learn to relax and have fun.
